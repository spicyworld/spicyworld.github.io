Banana Smoothie

© 2016 Spicy World, Published on: Sep 23, 2015

Whoever like me love to eat banana, this drink is for them. This is a very healthy drink for summer. This needs very few ingredients. Hope you will like it.




Ingredients

  • Two ripe bananas.
  • Hung curd 4 Teaspoon.
  • Whole milk 1 cup.
  • Sugar 3 Teaspoon.
  • Honey 1 Teaspoon.
  • Pinch of salt.
  • Ice cubes.

Steps

  • Take a blender. Put all the ingredients. Blend it very well.
  • Check the seasoning. Adjust the sweetness according to your taste.

Your banana smoothie is ready ...
Enjoy this as summer pleasure ...
