Chicken kali mirch

© 2016 Spicy World, Published on: Sep 13, 2015

I got this recipe from one famous cooking channel in youtube. So, let me thank them first. Now the main attraction of this chicken curry is its aroma and simpleness. When I was making it, the aroma from the spices made my husband and me very much impatient. But anyway we had to wait till dinner and enjoyed with hot roti a lot. Make this dish and I am sure it will become your favourite too.




Ingredients

  • Chicken 4-5 pieces
  • curd one medium bowl
  • black pepper corn 2 Teaspoon
  • five green cardamom
  • six cloves
  • one dry red chili
  • one onion chopped
  • two cloves garlic chopped
  • one inch ginger chopped
  • salt and sugar as per your taste
  • oil 3 Teaspoon
  • butter 1 Teaspoon
  • half glass warm water

Steps

  • Coarsely ground black pepper, green cardamom and cloves. Keep it aside.
  • Marinate the chicken pieces with curd and half of the coarsely grounded spices. Keep the marination in refrigerator for two hours.
  • After two hours take a pan. Heat oil and butter.
  • Add dry red chili and chopped garlic. Saute it for 1 minute.
  • Then add chopped onion. fry it for 5 minutes.
  • Now add the marinated chicken.
  • Add salt and sugar. Cook it for ten minutes.
  • Then add some water and chopped ginger.
  • Cover the pan and cook it 25 minutes in slow flame.
  • Check the seasoning and sprinkle the remaining grounded spices over the curry before turn off your heat.

Your Chicken kali mirch is ready.
Serve hot with roti, naan, paratha or kulcha.
