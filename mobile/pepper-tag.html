Golmorich Kochu / Taro root Pepper fry / Kalimirch Arbi (Veg Item)
One simple vegetable has so many names - kochu / arbi / taro root. My mom often makes this Golmorich kochu and our family, friends all love it. Whenever I go to ma's place, one of my demand is to have this dish. I don't feel the need of pairing it with anything like roti / rice. But you can have it with both. The recipe is very simple but don't underestimate the dish by its simplicity. First make it then let me know you like it or not.
