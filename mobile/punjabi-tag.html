Restaurant style Chicken Bharta (NonVeg Item)
Chicken Bharta with Butter naan is an unbeatable combo. Fragrant and creamy this gravy will always make your meal special. From younger to elder Chicken Bharta is loved by all. This is not my first post on Chicken Bharta, there is another delicious recipe of it which I made few months back. You can try that too, click the name to see that recipe kolkata style chicken bharta One day while cooking Butter Chicken, I thought of today's recipe. You will be hooked with this one after giving it a try.
