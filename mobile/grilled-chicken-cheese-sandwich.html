Grilled Chicken & Cheese Sandwich

© 2016 Spicy World, Published on: May 10, 2019

You don't have to be an expert to make this easy and simple grilled chicken and cheese sandwich! This chicken and cheese sandwich recipe is my family favorite. From younger to elder everybody love the taste and texture of it. For the flavor I marinated the chicken with some Tandoori masala and the taste came out really good. Do give this recipe a try and let me know how it turned out for you.




Ingredients

  • 8 soft white breads.
  • 300 grams of boneless chicken, cut into small chunks.
  • 1 Tablespoon of ginger garlic paste.
  • Some salt.
  • 1 Tablespoon of ground black pepper.
  • 2 Tablespoons of tandoori masala powder.
  • Half cup of thinly sliced onion and capsicums.
  • 4 Tablespoons of soft butter.
  • Any shredded or sliced cheese of your choice.
  • 4 Tablespoons of green chutney.
  • 4 Tablespoons of chili mayonnaise (mix chili sauce or ketchup with mayonnaise).
  • 2 Tablespoons of lemon juice.





Steps

Marinate the chicken with ginger garlic paste, salt, black pepper, tandoori masala and lemon juice. You can also add some red chili powder.
Keep it aside for 2 hours.
Heat 3 Tablespoons of oil in a pan.
Add marinated chicken and cook on high flame for 6-7 minutes.
Then add sliced onion and capsicums.
Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes on high flame.
Then turn off the heat.
Apply some soft butter on one side of breads.
Flip them over.
Apply some green chutney on one bread and some chili mayonnaise on the other.
Now add some cheese on one bread.
Then add 2-3 Tablespoons of chicken filling.
Again add some cheese over the chicken.
Cover it with another bread.
Now place the sandwich, buttered side down on medium hot pan.
Cook on medium flame for 2 minutes then flip it very carefully and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
Then take it out from the pan.
Cut it into half and serve.

Your grilled chicken & cheese sandwich is ready to serve.
Serve this hot with Green chutney or Ketchup ..
