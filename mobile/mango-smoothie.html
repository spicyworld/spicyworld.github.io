Mango Smoothie

© 2016 Spicy World, Published on: Sep 18, 2015

This is a very refreshing drink in summer. In India we call this Mango Lassi. My favourite summer fruit is mango. You can make many kinds of drink, dessert with mango. So let's try this.




Ingredients

  • Two ripe mangoes cut into small cubes.
  • Whole milk 1 cup.
  • Yogurt 1 cup.
  • Salt and sugar.
  • Two ice cubes.

Steps

  • Put the mango cubes in the freezer for 1 hour.
  • Take a blender put the mango cubes, milk, yogurt, pinch of salt and 3 Teaspoons sugar. Blend it very well.
  • Adjust the sugar with the sweetness of your mangoes.

Your mango smoothie is ready ...
Enjoy this with a ice cube on top of it ...
