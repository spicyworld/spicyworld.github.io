Chicken Satay - indo chinese style / Chicken Sathe

© 2016 Spicy World, Published on: Mar 30, 2020

Basically Chicken Satay is a dish from Thai cuisine, where small pieces of meat or fish cooked on a stick, and served with a spicy peanut sauce. But what today I am sharing is different than this one. Nowadays, if you see menu cards of Bengali weddings, Chicken Sathe will be the most common item. They serve Chicken Sathe as starter. First time I had it in a wedding and I was like wo! what a flavor and idea! In this Chicken Sathe recipe, marinated bite size chicken pieces will be skewered with onions and capsicums, then they will be batter fried until golden and lastly we will toss them in a spicy brown sauce. Believe me, you will be impressed with the end result. You can make it with fish or paneer also. Give this recipe a try and let me know how it turned out for you.




Ingredients

  • 500 grams of boneless chicken, cut into chunks.
  • 2 Tablespoons of ginger garlic green chili paste.
  • 1 Tablespoon of red chili powder.
  • 1 Tablespoon of black pepper powder.
  • 2 Tablespoons of lemon juice.
  • 1 small onion and 1 capsicum, cut into chunks.
  • 1 or 2 eggs.
  • Half cup of all purpose flour.
  • 1/4th cup of corn flour.
  • Salt and sugar as per your taste.
  • 1.5 Tablespoons of finely chopped ginger, garlic and green chilies.
  • 2 Tablespoons each of (tomato ketchup, soy sauce, red chili sauce).
  • 1 Tablespoon of oyster sauce (optional).
  • 1 Teaspoon of vinegar.
  • Some chopped green onion.
  • 2 Teaspoons of soy sauce.
  • Half cup of vegetable oil.
  • Bamboo skewers.
  • 2 Tablespoons of water.





Steps

Add ginger garlic green chili paste, salt, red chili powder, black pepper powder, 2 Teaspoons of soy sauce and lemon juice in the chicken. Mix well and keep aside for 1-2 hours.
In a mixing bowl, add all purpose flour, corn flour and some salt. Mix well.
Then add an egg and make a thick batter. If you need more egg then feel free to add.
Take a skewer, arrange onion, chicken and capsicum. Repeat the process in the same manner.
Dip one chicken skewer into the batter, rotate a little then drop it gently in hot oil.
Add 2 or 3 skewers at a time. Fry one side for 5 minutes then flip it to the other side and fry for 3-4 minutes.
Then take them out from oil.
In a small bowl, add all of the sauces along with little water. Mix well and keep aside.
In a separate pan, heat 2 Tablespoons of oil (in which you have fried the chicken).
Saute chopped ginger, garlic and green chilies.
Then add the sauce mixture and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add little salt and sugar, cook for a minute.
Then add the fried chicken skewers, mix well with the brown sauce for 1-2 minutes.
Lastly add vinegar and chopped green onion, toss well and turn off the heat.

Your chicken sathe is ready to serve.
Serve this hot as a starter or with fried rice ..
